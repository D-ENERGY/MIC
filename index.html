About Us

The Military Industrial Complex (MIC), Kaduna is a strategic defense and industrial manufacturing organization committed to strengthening national security through local production, advanced engineering solutions, research, industrial innovation, and technical manpower development.


MIC plays a vital role in supporting Nigeria’s industrial and defense sectors through indigenous manufacturing, equipment maintenance, research initiatives, and sustainable technological advancement.

Vision

To become a world-class defense and industrial manufacturing institution driven by innovation, self-reliance, and technological advancement.

Mission

  • Provide high-quality defense and industrial solutions.
  • Promote indigenous technology development.
  • Support national security through sustainable manufacturing.
  • Develop skilled technical manpower.
  • Foster research, innovation, and strategic partnerships.
